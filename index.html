SLAYARTWORX

Artist & creator of sci fantasy, cover art, concept work, character design, photography and video editing

Hi and welcome to the Slayartworx Showcase. My name is Robert Jason Slay; my friends call me Jason. I have been creating and drawing since the age of five years old. I have developed my style of art over the years by learning from and watching great artists, and following thier progress and careers. I am embarking on my journey into hopefully becoming a recognizable name within the art community. So what better place to start than my own webpage and gallery, committed to my particular style and perfered genre. I want to thank everyone for visiting. Even if you never commision me or buy any of my work, I appreciate you all.

I have been involved with small projects and collaborated with other creators Such as the independent film project; Vilikron Cronicles (1998), Joshuas Tree (1998), and smaller independent projects through out the years. Currently I am building up experience as a drone pilot and videographer. Well also learning photography. I am a student of art.
